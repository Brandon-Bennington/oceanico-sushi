About Oceanico Sushi Bar

Welcome to Oceanico Sushi Bar, where passion for Japanese cuisine meets the vibrant flavors of the ocean. Established in 2010, we have been serving the finest sushi and Japanese delicacies to our valued customers for over a decade.

Our team of experienced chefs, led by Master Sushi Chef Hiroshi Tanaka, takes pride in crafting each dish with precision and care. We source only the freshest ingredients, including daily catches from local fishermen, to ensure an authentic and delightful dining experience.

At Oceanico, we believe in blending traditional Japanese techniques with innovative culinary concepts. This fusion results in a menu that respects the roots of sushi-making while offering exciting new flavors for the modern palate.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our food. We strive to create a warm, inviting atmosphere where friends and families can gather to enjoy exceptional meals and create lasting memories.
